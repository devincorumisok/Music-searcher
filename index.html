Music Searcher

TIDAL: Offers HiFi (FLAC, 16-bit/44.1kHz) and Hi-Res (MQA, up to 24-bit/192kHz) audio. Known for high-quality streaming and lossless audio options.
QOBUZ: Supports Hi-Res audio (FLAC, up to 24-bit/192kHz). Known for offering lossless audio streaming and downloadable tracks.
DEEZER: Provides CD-quality (FLAC, 16-bit/44.1kHz) with their HiFi plan. Offers a wide variety of music at standard quality on other plans.
YANDEX: Provides a large music library with various streaming options and downloadable music. Known for its integration with Yandex services.
AMAZON MUSIC: Offers a wide selection of music streaming, including HD and Ultra HD audio options for high-fidelity listening.
SPOTIFY: Known for its vast library and personalized playlists. Offers both free (ad-supported) and premium subscription options.
